📖 How to Play

🎯 Basic Rules

Take turns firing at the enemy grid. Sink all 5 enemy ships to win.
Ships: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (2).
Hit = 🔴, Miss = ·, Sunk = entire ship turns red.

⚡ Charge System

You gain +1 charge each time one of your ships is hit (max 6).
Spend charge to use special abilities instead of a normal shot.
After any action, press "End Turn" to confirm and pass to the opponent.

💥 Special Abilities

💣
Mine2 ⚡
Place a mine on your own board (max 2 active). If an enemy hits it, the attack misses AND a random backfire shot fires back at them (50% damage chance). Mine is consumed on trigger.
📡
Sonar Scan3 ⚡
Target any missed tile. Scans a 3×3 area and reports how many ship segments are hiding there (no positions revealed).
🎯
Precision Strike3 ⚡
Guaranteed hit if a ship exists on the target tile. Can't be used twice in a row.
💣
Depth Bomb4 ⚡
Hits the target tile plus the 4 orthogonal neighbours (cross pattern, 5 tiles total).
✈️
Airstrike5 ⚡
Hits 3 tiles in a straight line. Click to pick orientation (horizontal or vertical). Starts at target and extends in chosen direction.
